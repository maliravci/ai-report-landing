Features

Everything You Need for
Automated Reporting

A practical report builder designed for real workflows: extraction, validation, correction, and export.

Template-Aware Extraction

The app reads your template structure, detects expected fields, and extracts matching values from source documents automatically.

Confidence Scoring

Each field includes a confidence score so teams can prioritize review and focus attention where risk is highest.

Field-Level Editing

Adjust any value manually, lock approved fields, and rerun extraction only for fields that still need refinement.

Reliable Export

Generate complete .docx or .xlsx reports while preserving original layout, formulas, and formatting standards.

Multilingual Teams

Use the platform in 24 localized interfaces to support distributed teams and region-specific reporting processes.

Transparent Review Trail

Track extracted values, confidence history, and manual overrides so the final report remains explainable and auditable.
