Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how ReportDrafter handles your information.

Last updated: February 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create a ReportDrafter account, we collect your name, email address, organization name, and password. This information is used to provision your workspace and authenticate your sessions.

Uploaded Documents

You may upload report templates and source documents to the platform. These files are processed server-side for AI field extraction. We do not use uploaded documents to train machine learning models or share them with third parties.

Usage Data

We automatically collect technical data including browser type, device type, IP address, pages visited, and feature usage patterns. This data helps us improve the product and diagnose issues.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for session management and authentication. We may use analytics cookies (e.g., page view counts) that do not track individuals across sites. You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the ReportDrafter service
  • To authenticate users and secure workspaces
  • To process documents and generate AI-extracted reports
  • To send transactional emails (e.g., password resets, usage alerts)
  • To aggregate anonymized usage statistics for product improvement
  • To respond to support requests and communicate product updates

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Uploaded documents are stored in isolated, encrypted storage buckets with access restricted to your workspace. We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing.

4. Data Retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your active subscription. Uploaded documents are automatically deleted 90 days after processing unless you configure longer retention in your workspace settings. You can manually delete any document at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Cloud Infrastructure: Microsoft Azure for compute, storage, and networking
  • AI Processing: Azure OpenAI Service for document field extraction
  • Email: Transactional email provider for account notifications
  • Analytics: Privacy-focused analytics for aggregate usage metrics

None of these providers have access to your document content beyond what is necessary for processing.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities
  • Withdraw consent where processing is consent-based

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@reportdrafter.com.

7. Children's Privacy

ReportDrafter is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you via email or an in-app banner. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, reach out to us:

  • Email: privacy@reportdrafter.com
  • Address: ReportDrafter, Inc. — 548 Market Street, Suite 46382, San Francisco, CA 94104
