About ReportDrafter

We're building practical AI tools that help teams complete reports faster — with confidence and control.

Our Mission

Operations, compliance, and finance teams spend hours manually transferring data between source documents and report templates. ReportDrafter was built to reduce that burden. Our mission is to make document-based reporting faster, more accurate, and fully transparent — so teams can focus on analysis instead of data entry.

What We Believe

  • AI should assist, not replace. Our platform is designed around human review. Every AI-extracted value comes with a confidence score so you decide what to trust and what to correct.
  • Transparency builds trust. We show exactly how confident the model is on every field. No black-box magic — just clear indicators that help you work faster without sacrificing accuracy.
  • Simplicity wins. Upload a template, add your source files, review the results, and download a finished report. No complex setup, no training required.

Our Story

ReportDrafter started in 2024 when our founding team noticed that compliance officers across financial services were spending 30–40% of their time copying data between documents. Despite advances in AI, most tools either lacked transparency or required extensive customization.

We set out to build a report automation platform that works out of the box: upload your existing templates, point it at your source documents, and get back structured results with clear confidence ratings. No AI expertise needed.

Since launch, ReportDrafter has processed over 120,000 reports across 28 countries, supporting 21 languages and teams of all sizes — from solo consultants to enterprise compliance departments.
